Durbuy is often called the
smallest city in the world and is
known for its charming medieval
streets along the Ourthe River in
the Belgian Ardennes. It is a
popular destination for outdoor
activities like hiking and kayaking,
as well as cozy cafés and local
restaurants.

Sail on the clear water of the
Ourthe river, surrounded by the
natural beauty of the Ardennes.
Paddle through the breathtaking
landscapes of the region, while
enjoying a moment of relaxation
and adventure with family and
friends.
